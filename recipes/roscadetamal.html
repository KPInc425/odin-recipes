Rosca De Tamal by Cooking Con Claudia

Description

Tamales too much work, can't find the motivation? Never made them before because theyy seem intimidating? Well look no further than this delicious recipe making that same tradional goodness, in a not so traditional manner. Paired with all the fixings sourcream, guac and a nice cabbage slaw, this dinner can't be beat!

This one isn't really for the keto gang, but the meat can't be beat and there will be ample on the side to fix up a nice salad, or throw into a low carb tortilla!

Ingredients

Protein

  • 3 - 4 pounds Pork Roast/Butt
  • 1/2 an Onion
  • 4 Garlic Cloves
  • 2 tsp Pepper Corn
  • 8 Cups Water
  • Salt to Taste

Chile Colorado

  • 10 New Mexico Chile Pods(Cleaned)
  • 2 Chile Anchos(Cleaned)
  • 5 - 6 Chile Arbol(Optional)
  • 7 small Tomatillos
  • 4 Garlic Cloves
  • 3/4th tsp Cumin Seeds
  • Salt(Claudia Suggest 2 tsp)

Masa

  • 4 Cups Instant Corn Masa
  • 1 Cup Lard or Shortening
  • 2 tsp Baking Powder
  • 6 Cups Liquid (3 Cups Water, 3 Cups Broth)
    *You can use all 6 cups of broth, but lower shortening amount to prevent oily masa*

Misc

  • 4 - 5 hydrated Corn Husks (Soak Overnight, or with HOT water while meat cooks)
  • Claudia says "1 Serving of Love"

Tips

  • Taste Masa for salt after adding all ingredients
  • Make sure Masa floats in water, to ensure moist masa
  • Oil Bundt pan

Steps

Chile Colorado

  1. Put all Protein ingredients into Instant Pot.
  2. Use Instant Pot Meat/Stew settings and cook for 1 and 1/2 hours.
  3. When finished, release steam after 15 minutes.
  4. Remove meat.
  5. Strain and Save 5 cups of Broth.
  6. Put all Chile and Tomatillos ingredients for Chile Colorado into remaining broth left in Instant Pot. And let sit for about 10 minutes.
  7. While you wait, shred meat.
  8. Once meat is shredded, remove ingredients from broth and put into blending appuratus.
  9. Add remaining ingredients from Chile Colorado section. (garlic, cumin, salt)
  10. Add 2 Cups of reserved Broth to blender. Blend until Smooth.
  11. In Warm pan over Medium Heat add the sauce and let simmer for about 5 Minutes. Taste for sauce and add if needed.
  12. Reserve 1 Cup of Sauce
  13. Add Shredded Meat. Lower heat to Low and let simmer for 3-5 minutes.

Masa

  1. Mix 3 Cups of reserved Broth with 3 Cups of Water
  2. Put 1 Cup or 8oz of Lard into mixing appuratus
  3. Put mixer on Medium Speed for 5 Minutes, until creamy.
  4. Reduce mixer speed and Add 2 tsp Baking Powder
  5. Slowly start adding Masa and Broth mixture to mixer alternating
  6. Once everything is in, increase mixer speed back to Medium and allow to mix for 15 minutes.
  7. Check Masa buy taking a small piece and dropping it in water to see if it floats.
  8. Let Masa rest for about 20 Minutes
  9. Meanwhile, grease bundt pan. Get Steamer Pot ready. (or any pot large enough to hold water and the bundt pan) Add enough water that it touches the bottom of bundt pan.

Assembly

  1. Pour Half of the Masa into greased Bundt Pan.
  2. Try and create channel in center of "cake" all the way around where you will be placing meat.
  3. Place Meat in channel on top of Masa.
  4. Once Meat is placed, pour remaining Masa over the Meat.
  5. Place Hydrated Corn Husks over open end of bundt pan
  6. Cover top with Foil

Cooking

  1. Place Bundt Pan back over water in Large Pot.
  2. Cover with kitchen towel, position carefully to prevent fire.
  3. Turn up heat to Medium Low and allow to cook for 55-60 Minutes
  4. Check if Masa is cooked, when ready flip bundt pan over onto hard surface.
  5. Garnish with Reserved Sauce.

(Feel free to change up the Seasonings/Meat and/or toss with your favorite sauce.)
