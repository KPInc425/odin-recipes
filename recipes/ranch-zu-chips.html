Ranch Zucchini Chips

Description

Make sure you slice them nice and thin to get that crispy texture just like a potato chip. The easist method would be thin slices on a mandolin. With a little finesse and some yummy spices, you won't even miss to the potato.

You will be up to snuff with your keto friends without making them jelous of others eating more traditional snacks. With a little ranch seasioning these delicious chips will stand up to them cool ranch doritos and keep the fussiest of eaters satisfied.

Ingredients

  • Cooking Spray
  • 2 Zucchini (sliced very thinly into coins)
  • 1 teaspoon Ranch Seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon Oregano (dried)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Freshly Ground Pepper (to taste)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375Â°.
  2. Thinly slice zucchini into chips and pat excess moisture dry with paper towel.
  3. In large bowl, toss zucchini with oil.
  4. Toss in ranch seasoning, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  5. Place in a sigle layer on baking basket.
  6. Air fry 6 minutes, then flip and cook another 6 minutes.
  7. Remove golden chips and continue cooking the rest of chips in 2 minute intervals (Shake the basket every minute for even crisp)
