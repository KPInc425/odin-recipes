Lemon Peppered Air Fried Wings

Description

No need to go out for that deliscious plate of quality chicken wings! These zesty and tangy wings are sure to bring a smile to the face of the most diehard wing connoiseur. Pair with a tasty homemade ranch, Wings are in tonight!

Even the Keto gang can enjoy these wings and will thank you when you pair it with the Crispy Ranch Zucchini Chips for a low carb sinfully delicious duo, that is assure to keep them asking for more!

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds Chicken Wings
  • 3 Tablespoons Olive Oil
  • 1/4th cup Lemon Pepper Seasoning
  • 2 teaspoons Baking Powder (NOT SODA, optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
  2. Pat chicken wings dry with paper towel.
  3. Place chicken wings into a large bowl
  4. Add oil and mis to coat
  5. Sprinkle lemon pepper seasoning over the chicken and rub into the skin all over.
  6. Toss with Baking Powder, if using
  7. Arrange the wings on racks in a single layer, without touching.
  8. Air fry for 15 minutes @ 250°F.
  9. Flip the wings over.
  10. Increase airfrying temp 430°F (MAX if lower).
  11. Air fry for about 15 to 20 additional minutes, until wings are done and crispy.

(Feel free to change up the seasoning and/or toss with your favorite sauce.)
